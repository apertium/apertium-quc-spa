Ri areʼ kubʼij chrech ri ixoq: «Numiʼal, ri akojonik xatukolo, jat pa ri nimalaj utzil».
Ri areʼ, pa taq ri uqʼijil ri ukʼaslemal ri ubʼaqil, xuta rukʼ sibʼalaj raqoj chiʼaj e rukʼ sibʼalaj uwaʼl uwach chrech ri Areʼ, ri kkowinik kukol ri areʼ chuwach ri kamikal
Ri Jesus kubʼij chkech: «¿Joropaʼ kaxlanwa kʼo iwukʼ ix?». «Wuqubʼ kecha ri eʼareʼ e kebʼ oxibʼ alaj taq kär».
Kʼatekʼuriʼ, chi chanim kʼo chi kʼakʼ uwoqʼoch e kuterneʼbʼej ubʼi ri areʼ pa ri bʼe.

Ri Jesus kubʼij: «Nutat, chasachaʼ bʼaʼ kimak ri eʼareʼ: man ketaʼm taj ri tajin kkibʼano». 
Ri eʼareʼ kketzʼabʼej ubʼik su ri ujachik kkibʼan ubʼi chkiwach ri ratzʼyaq.
Ri winaq ekʼo chilaʼ tajin kekaʼyik. 
Ri ekinimal tinimit kketzʼabʼej uwach ri areʼ kkibʼij: «¡Xeʼukol e nikʼaj chik, chukoloʼ bʼa ribʼ ri areʼ, we are ri Kristo rech ri Dyos, ri chaʼom!».
